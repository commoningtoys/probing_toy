Welcome to the amtli-manager. This first thinking toy will give you a glimpse of how work influences a community, and how group dynamics work. You can choose to participate in the community as a single agent or as group. The app will give you sensorial feedback on how the community is doing, and will ask you to intervene in some matters. Remember that to achieve something as a solo agent is way harder than doing it as a group.
